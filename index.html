Hamilton Inn

Hamilton Inn is a website for guests to make inquiries to stay in a five-story townhouse in Manhattan, New York. Using Bootstrap and SCSS, I responsively built and designed haminn.com. The blueprint of the house was built with D3.js and SVGs. Using Google Forms, visitors can make inquiries based on their travel plans. These inquiries are sent via email and through slack to the administrators.

Portfolio - Matthew Steele

This portfolio is built for Matthew Steele, an urban economist with data visualization skills.
